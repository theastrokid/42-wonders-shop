About the Author

Damon Scotting

Damon is an astronomy communicator and creator driven by a simple belief: the night sky belongs to everyone.

Through astrophotography, science communication, and a genuine passion for making the cosmos accessible, Damon created this book to share the wonders that keep him looking up — night after night.

42 Wonders of Our Night Sky is the result of that passion: a book designed to inspire anyone to step outside, look up, and see the universe with fresh eyes.
